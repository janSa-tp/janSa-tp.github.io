This is my updated version of blinry's cheat sheet. With my update, I tried to go more indepth in each of the categories, as well as add missing explanations such as preverbs.

These cheat sheets are meant to be a quick and easy to use resource for refreshers on Toki Pona. In the original version, it only uses one piece of paper, with grammar on one side and vocabulary on the other. With the 2 sheet version, one piece of paper is grammar, and the other is vocab.
